Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PayPe Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("BankMe", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address and mobile number
  • Aadhaar number (last 4 digits only, for AEPS)
  • PAN number for KYC compliance
  • Bank account details for money transfer services
  • Shop details including address and business certificates
  • Biometric data (processed locally, not stored)

Transaction Information

  • Transaction history and amounts
  • Service usage data
  • RTAI risk scores and compliance logs
  • Device information and IP addresses

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our fintech services
  • To process transactions and payments
  • To comply with RBI, UIDAI and other regulatory requirements
  • To detect and prevent fraud using RTAI technology
  • To send transaction receipts and service notifications
  • To verify your identity through eKYC
  • To generate invoices and maintain audit logs

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Banking partners for transaction processing (Eko India, partner banks)
  • UIDAI for Aadhaar verification
  • NPCI for UPI and BBPS transactions
  • IRCTC for train bookings
  • Insurance and loan partners for respective services
  • Regulatory authorities as required by law
  • Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • RTAI encryption for all transactions
  • Firebase Firestore with security rules
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • Screen capture protection in our app
  • RTAI audit logs for all activities
  • Regular security assessments

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as required by RBI and other regulatory guidelines. Transaction records are kept for a minimum of 5 years as per RBI guidelines. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to request data deletion (subject to regulatory requirements)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our web application uses localStorage and session cookies for authentication and user preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

BankMe services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
